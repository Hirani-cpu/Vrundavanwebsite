Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM (noon) and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Is parking available at the resort?

Yes, we provide free parking for all our guests. Valet parking service is also available on request.

Do you allow pets?

Yes, pets are allowed with prior notice. Please inform us during booking if you're bringing a pet.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a 50% charge. No-shows will be charged in full.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort premises free of charge for all guests.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport pickup and drop services on request. Additional charges apply.

Are meals included in the room rate?

Complimentary breakfast is included with all room bookings. Lunch and dinner can be ordered from our restaurant.

Can I customize the event package?

Yes, all event packages are fully customizable. Our event coordinator will work with you to create a package that suits your needs and budget.
